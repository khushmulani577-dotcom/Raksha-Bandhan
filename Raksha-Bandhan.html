The Essence of Raksha Bandhan

A Sacred Bond

Raksha Bandhan symbolizes the purest form of love between siblings. The rakhi thread represents protection, while the sweet exchange signifies mutual affection.

Our Special Ritual

Every year, we carry forward our unique tradition of writing heartfelt letters to each other along with the rakhi exchange - a keepsake of our growing bond.

Modern Meaning

Beyond the rituals, Raksha Bandhan reminds us to appreciate our relationship throughout the year - through calls, messages, and most importantly, being there when it matters.
